Fortnite Battle Royale

What is it?

Fortnite is a first person Battle Royale shooting game where you start out in a Bus in the sky,and then you have to jump out of it and land in many different places. In those places,there are chests,guns,and suplies that you can pick up to kill other people. There are Solos,Duos,and Squads and special events like snipers only,50vs50, and many other fun ones. each game, you play against people that are playing world wide.

You always spawn in a game with an axe so you can break stuff to get materials to build forts and obstacles for people to get through to then get to you.

The circle is the place you have to be in so that you don't get into the storm. If you get caught in a storm thats getting smaller quicker, you lose health little by little, but if you are caught in a fast storm, then you lose health very quickely. It is possible to dye because you are in the storm for to

Chests can be opened, and can give you anything in the game ranging from materials to guns of any type.

Guns are key to surviving and getting kills in this game. ARs and explosives are good with medium range kills,and destroying bases of enemies. Snipers are for long range kills. Hand canons and pistols are very useful for close or medium range unseen kills. Shotguns are very useful for close range shootouts.

How good guns, meds, and almost everything in Fortnite is determined by 5 different colors. The difference between colors vary, for example a green AR has better accuracy, and does more damage than a grey AR.
