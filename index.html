Fortnite Battle Royale

What is it?

Fortnite is a first person Battle Royale shooting game where you start out in a Bus in the sky,and then you have to jump out of it and land in many different places. In those places,there are chests,guns,and suplies that you can pick up to kill other people. There are Solos,Duos,and Squads and specialevents like snipers only,50vs50,

You always spawn in a game with an axe so you can break stuff to get materials to build forts and obstacles for people to get through to then get to you.

Chests can be opened, and can give you anything in the game ranging from materials to guns of any type.

Guns are key to surviving and getting kills in this game. ARs and explosives are good with medium range kills,and destroying bases of enemies. Snipers are for long range kills. Hand canons and pistols are very useful for close or medium range unseen kills. Shotguns are very useful for close range shootouts.
